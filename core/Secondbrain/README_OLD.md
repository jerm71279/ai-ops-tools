# Second Brain

A personal knowledge management system for capturing, organizing, and connecting ideas.

## Structure

- **inbox/** - Quick captures and unprocessed notes
- **notes/** - Permanent notes and atomic ideas (Zettelkasten-style)
- **projects/** - Active project documentation and notes
- **areas/** - Ongoing responsibilities and areas of interest
- **resources/** - Reference materials, articles, and research
- **archive/** - Completed projects and outdated information
- **daily/** - Daily notes and journal entries
- **templates/** - Note templates for consistency

## Workflow

1. **Capture** - Add quick thoughts to `inbox/`
2. **Process** - Review inbox regularly and move notes to appropriate folders
3. **Connect** - Link related notes together
4. **Review** - Weekly review of projects and areas

## Getting Started

Start by creating your first note in the inbox or use one of the templates!
